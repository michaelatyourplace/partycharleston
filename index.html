Don't Forget the Beaches!

The party starts long before sunset. Charleston is minutes away from world-class beaches that are destinations in their own right.

Head to Folly Beach for a laid-back, bohemian surf vibe with lively beachfront bars, or visit Isle of Palms for a slightly more upscale scene with great restaurants and wide, sandy shores. They're the perfect places to day-drink, catch some sun, and get the party started early.

Top 5 Reasons Charleston is Your Ultimate Party Destination

The Legendary Bar Scene

From the bustling nightlife of King Street with its rooftop bars and high-energy clubs to the cozy, historic pubs in the French Quarter, Charleston offers a bar for every mood and every crowd. The options are endless and the drinks are always flowing.

Day Parties & Beach Vibes

The party doesn't wait for the sun to go down! Head to Folly Beach or Isle of Palms for legendary beach bars and day-drinking culture. Boat parties on the harbor are a local favorite, offering booze cruises with stunning views of the city. Everyone goes to the beaches at least once while in the lowcountry.

World-Class Events & Festivals

Charleston's calendar is packed with reasons to celebrate. From the Spoleto Festival USA to the Charleston Wine + Food Festival, there's always a large-scale event happening that brings an electric, celebratory energy to the entire city. Even down to local events like dance shows, theater or private party performances like bachelorette party dancer or Dudleys!

More Than Just a Party: Charleston, The Traveler's Choice

While the nightlife is second to none, it's just one piece of the puzzle.

Historic Charm

Cobblestone streets, horse-drawn carriages, and pastel antebellum houses make it one of the most beautiful and historic cities in America.

Culinary Capital

Charleston is a foodie paradise, boasting James Beard award-winning chefs and a culinary scene that's celebrated globally. It's the perfect place to refuel after a long night out.

Easy to Reach

With a major international airport (CHS) and its position as a top-tier destination, people from all over the world flock to Charleston for its unique blend of history, food, and unforgettable party atmosphere.
