The Perfect Charleston Bachelorette Party

Your guide to the most Instagrammable, fun-filled, and unforgettable weekend for the bride-to-be.

Charleston is the bachelorette party capital of the South for a reason. It offers the perfect mix of picturesque charm, incredible food, beautiful beaches, and a nightlife scene that’s second to none. Here’s how to plan the ultimate send-off for the bride.

Daytime Fun: Sun, Shopping & Sips

  • Beach Day at Folly or Sullivan's: Pack your coolers and cutest swimsuits for a day on the sand. Folly Beach offers a funky, laid-back vibe while Sullivan's Island is a bit more relaxed and upscale.
  • Private Boat Charter: Nothing says "bachelorette party" like a private boat day. Cruise the harbor, spot dolphins, and pop champagne with your best friends.
  • Shopping on King Street: From high-end boutiques to charming local shops, a stroll down King Street is a must for some retail therapy.
  • Spa Day or Wellness Session: Unwind with massages at a luxury spa or book a private yoga session for the group to relax before the night's festivities.

Essential Eats & Drinks

  • The Darling Oyster Bar: An iconic, Instagram-worthy spot. The raw bar is incredible, and the bloody mary is a work of art. A must-do for brunch or dinner.
  • Camellias Champagne Lounge: Located in Hotel Bennett, this stunning, all-pink lounge is the perfect place for a classy glass of bubbly and gorgeous photos.
  • Uptown Social: A lively, sprawling bar with frozen drinks, live music, and a huge rooftop. It’s a guaranteed party atmosphere any time of day.

Rooftop Toasts: The Best Views

  • The Citrus Club: Atop The Dewberry hotel, this chic, tropical-themed bar offers the best panoramic views of the city. Perfect for sunset cocktails.
  • Fiat Lux: Another elegant option at Hotel Bennett overlooking Marion Square. Ideal for a sophisticated, pre-dinner drink.

Late Night: Dancing & Entertainment

  • Trio: Charleston's premier nightclub, featuring top-tier DJs, bottle service, and multiple dance floors for a true clubbing experience.
  • The Commodore: A funky, retro jazz club with amazing live bands that will have your whole group dancing all night long.
  • Drag Brunch at Dudley's on Ann: For a fun and fabulous weekend activity, book a drag brunch. It’s an incredibly popular bachelorette choice, combining great food, drinks, and unforgettable entertainment.
