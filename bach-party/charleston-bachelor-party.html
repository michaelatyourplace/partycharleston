The Holy City Send-Off

Charleston Bachelor Party

Southern charm by day, day club by afternoon, and a King Street bar crawl that runs till 2AM. Here's the whole weekend, planned out.

The Arthur Ravenel Jr. Bridge — your backdrop for the weekend
Gentlemen, Welcome

Southern Hospitality Meets Serious Nightlife

Charleston's bachelor party scene has changed fast. On top of the historic pub crawls and rooftop bars, the Holy City now has a full daytime day club with cabanas and international DJs, a wagyu-burger-and-live-music bar that's the new go-to before a night out, and a wave of new restaurants worth building the whole weekend around. This is the current version of that weekend — the classics that still hold up, plus what just opened.

01

The Lineup

Charleston runs on the tide — so does your weekend. Here's how the day breaks down.

Fuel Up

Biscuits, shrimp and grits, or bottomless mimosas before the group scatters to boats, the beach, or the golf course.

Afternoon

Water & Sun

Night

Live music at On Air, rooftop cocktails, then the crawl down King Street until last call at 2AM.

02

New This Season

The two spots every Charleston bachelor party is asking about right now.
New Opening Day Club

Palma Day Club

A Hamptons-style pool party transplanted onto Meeting Street. Palma runs poolside sets from touring house DJs every Saturday through Labor Day, with a full cocktail bar and a menu built around a CHS Wagyu smash burger. VIP cabanas hold up to ten guests and include a dedicated server and bottle service — the move for a bachelor party that wants daytime energy before the real night starts.

1705 Meeting St  ·  Noon–8PM Saturdays  ·  21+, cabanas by reservation
New Opening Live Music

On Air

Half live-music bar, half burger spot, all crowd. The lineup rotates constantly, but the reason everyone keeps coming back is the same: a wagyu double smash burger with grilled onions and bacon that's become the thing people talk about the next morning. A solid first or last stop for a bachelor party crew that wants music without the cover charge of a full club.

Charleston  ·  Live music nightly  ·  Book a table on Resy
