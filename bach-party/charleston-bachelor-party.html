Your Complete Charleston Bachelor Party Guide

Everything you need to know for an epic weekend in the Holy City

Daytime Activities

Folly Beach & Isle of Palms

Hit the pristine beaches just minutes from downtown Charleston. Folly Beach offers a laid-back surf town vibe with beach bars, volleyball, and great waves. Isle of Palms provides a more upscale beach experience with calmer waters perfect for swimming and relaxing. Both beaches have rental options for chairs, umbrellas, and water sports equipment. Don't miss the iconic Folly Beach Pier for fishing and sunset views, or grab cold beers and fish tacos at the beach bars along Center Street.

Boat Tours & Charter Boats

Experience Charleston from the water with private charter boats for fishing, harbor cruises, or sunset tours. Deep-sea fishing charters are perfect for reeling in king mackerel, cobia, and grouper. For something more relaxed, book a harbor tour to see Fort Sumter, the USS Yorktown, and dolphins playing in the wake. Private yacht charters offer luxury experiences with open bars and catering. Sandbar parties are legendary - anchor at a popular sandbar, bring your cooler, and join other boaters for an unforgettable afternoon on the water.

Downtown Charleston

Explore one of America's most historic and beautiful cities. Walk the cobblestone streets of Rainbow Row, tour historic homes and plantations, or take a carriage ride through the French Quarter. Check out the historic City Market for local crafts and food. Visit The Battery and White Point Garden for stunning waterfront views. The area around King Street offers world-class shopping, galleries, and hundreds of restaurants. Don't miss a ghost tour at night to hear spooky tales of Charleston's haunted past, or a historic pub crawl to experience taverns dating back to the 1700s.

Fun Activities

Topgolf

Located in Mount Pleasant, Topgolf offers climate-controlled hitting bays with microchipped golf balls that track your accuracy and distance. It's perfect for groups of all skill levels - compete in games, enjoy food and drinks delivered to your bay, and experience this high-tech driving range entertainment. They offer bachelor party packages that include private bays, food credits, and unlimited gameplay. Great for afternoon or evening entertainment with the guys, combining golf, games, food, and drinks all in one spot. Reserve your bay in advance, especially for weekend prime time slots.

Chucktown Axe Throwing

Release your inner lumberjack at Charleston's premier axe throwing venue. Professional instructors teach you proper technique before you compete in tournament-style competitions with your crew. They offer private lanes for bachelor parties where you can bring your own drinks and food. Perfect icebreaker activity that gets everyone laughing and competing. Located in Park Circle, it's a unique Charleston experience that combines skill, competition, and fun. Book a 90-minute session for your group and they'll provide everything you need including axes, targets, and instruction. It's one of the most memorable and talked-about activities you can do for a bachelor party.

Bar Crawls for Bachelors

Charleston's nightlife is legendary. Here's your ultimate guide to the best bars for your bachelor party crew:

The Rooftop Bar at The Vendue

Start your night with stunning 360-degree views of Charleston from this iconic rooftop bar. Craft cocktails, outdoor fire pits, and the best sunset views in the city. Perfect for the group photo before hitting the town.

Republic Garden & Lounge

Upscale nightclub with live DJs, VIP bottle service, and a sophisticated crowd. Multi-level venue with indoor and outdoor spaces. Great for dancing and partying late into the night. Book a VIP table for your crew.

Proof

Speakeasy-style bar with expert mixologists crafting amazing cocktails. Upscale but not pretentious, with a great whiskey selection. Perfect mid-crawl stop for quality drinks in a cool atmosphere.

Rec Room

Massive arcade bar with vintage games, skeeball, foosball, and more. Casual vibe with plenty of beers on tap. Great spot to let loose and get competitive with the guys while enjoying affordable drinks.

Barrel

Premium bourbon and whiskey bar with over 400 selections. Intimate setting perfect for groups who appreciate fine spirits. Great cocktails and knowledgeable bartenders who can recommend the perfect dram.

Mynt

High-energy nightclub on Upper King Street with top DJs and dancing until 2 AM. Modern club atmosphere with bottle service available. This is where you end your night when you want to keep the party going.

Burns Alley Tavern

Dive bar with character located in a historic alley. No-frills environment with strong drinks and a loyal local crowd. Perfect for a more authentic Charleston bar experience away from the tourist spots.

The Cocktail Club

Craft cocktail bar with creative drinks and a stylish interior. Great for starting the night or a more sophisticated drinking experience. Excellent bartenders who take their craft seriously.

Tommy Condon's Irish Pub

Traditional Irish pub with live music most nights. Large groups welcome with plenty of seating. Great pub food and Guinness on tap. Family-owned since 1987, it's a Charleston institution.

Huriyali

Late-night garden bar and restaurant open until 2 AM. Perfect for when you need food during your bar crawl. Outdoor seating, good vibes, and tasty bites to keep the party going.

Pro Bar Crawl Tips
  • Stay hydrated - alternate water between bars
  • Use Uber/Lyft - parking is difficult and expensive downtown
  • Most bars don't have cover charges except on special event nights
  • King Street is the main bar crawl hub - everything is walkable
  • Bring cash for tips and smaller bars that may not take cards
  • Start around 8-9 PM, bars stay open until 2 AM

Best Restaurants in Charleston

Fuel up with the finest cuisine throughout the day!

Breakfast

Hominy Grill

Southern Breakfast

Classic Charleston breakfast spot famous for their shrimp and grits, big nasty biscuit, and perfect sweet potato pancakes. Casual, welcoming atmosphere perfect for groups recovering from the night before. Expect a wait on weekends, but it's worth it for authentic Lowcountry breakfast.

$$

Callie's Hot Little Biscuit

Biscuits & Coffee

Grab-and-go biscuit shop with incredible handmade biscuits served with various toppings from fried chicken to pimento cheese. Multiple locations around Charleston. Perfect for a quick, delicious start to your day before hitting activities. Their buttermilk biscuits are legendary.

$

Toast All Day

Brunch Spot

Modern brunch restaurant with creative breakfast options and bottomless mimosas. Great for groups with diverse tastes - everything from healthy avocado toast to indulgent chicken and waffles. Fun, energetic atmosphere perfect for bachelor party mornings.

$$

Page's Okra Grill

Lowcountry Breakfast

Family-owned restaurant serving authentic Lowcountry breakfast since 1997. Known for massive portions, friendly service, and homestyle cooking. Try their crab omelet or country fried steak. Cash only, so come prepared. True local favorite off the tourist path.

$

Lunch

Lewis Barbecue

Texas BBQ

Award-winning Texas-style BBQ with massive portions and incredible brisket. Get there early because they sell out. Casual picnic table seating perfect for groups. Craft beer selection and great sides. The brisket and beef ribs are legendary - easily some of the best BBQ in the South.

$$

Fleet Landing

Waterfront Seafood

Waterfront restaurant on a restored naval building with stunning harbor views. Perfect lunch spot with fresh seafood, famous shrimp and grits, and cold beers. Large group-friendly with indoor and outdoor seating. Great place to relax and enjoy Charleston's waterfront.

$$-$$$

Boxcar Betty's

Fried Chicken

No-frills fried chicken sandwich spot that's become a Charleston institution. Simple menu done exceptionally well - fried chicken, slaw, pickles, sauce on a bun. Quick service, casual atmosphere, perfect for lunch between activities. The Betty sandwich is a must-try.

$

EVO Pizzeria

Pizza & Craft Beer

Awesome pizza spot with creative pies, huge portions, and excellent craft beer selection. Located in Park Circle, it's perfect for casual group lunches. Try their monthly specials and don't skip the wings. Great outdoor patio for nice weather.

$$

Butcher & Bee

Mediterranean Sandwiches

Trendy spot with incredible sandwiches, salads, and small plates. Known for their whipped feta, lamb burger, and creative vegetarian options. Hip atmosphere with communal seating. Great for groups who want something fresh and different.

$$

Dinner

Hall's Chophouse

Premium Steakhouse

Charleston's premier steakhouse with dry-aged beef, impeccable service, and live gospel music on Sundays. Perfect for the bachelor party dinner with an extensive wine list and premium cuts. VIP private dining rooms available for groups. Dress code enforced - jackets recommended. An experience worth the splurge.

$$$$

The Macintosh

Bourbon & Modern Southern

Upscale restaurant with innovative Southern cuisine and one of the best bourbon selections in Charleston. Chef-driven menu that changes seasonally. Sophisticated but not stuffy atmosphere. Great for groups who appreciate creative food and premium spirits. The duck and oysters are standouts.

$$$-$$$$

Hank's Seafood

Upscale Seafood

Classic Charleston seafood restaurant in a historic building in the heart of downtown. Known for fresh oysters, she-crab soup, and an extensive seafood menu. Upscale but welcoming atmosphere perfect for special dinners. Great raw bar and wine list. Make reservations for large groups.

$$$

FIG

Farm-to-Table

Award-winning restaurant focusing on seasonal, locally-sourced ingredients. Menu changes regularly based on what's fresh. Intimate setting perfect for groups who want an elevated dining experience. Reservation essential. Known as one of Charleston's best restaurants - book well in advance.

$$$$

The Rooftop Bar & Restaurant

Rooftop Dining

Stunning rooftop restaurant with 360-degree views of Charleston. Perfect for dinner and drinks with the guys while watching the sunset. Shareable plates, craft cocktails, and an energetic atmosphere. Great spot to start your evening before hitting the bars. Make reservations for the best tables.

$$$

Obstinate Daughter

Italian - Sullivan's Island

Creative Italian restaurant on Sullivan's Island with wood-fired pizzas, house-made pasta, and fresh seafood. Trendy spot worth the drive from downtown. Great for groups with its communal atmosphere and shareable dishes. Try the pork shoulder pizza and any pasta special.

$$$
