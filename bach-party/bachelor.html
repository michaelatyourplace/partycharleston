The Ultimate Charleston Bachelor Party

Your definitive guide to an epic last bash in the Holy City.

Charleston is a top-tier destination for a reason. It perfectly blends world-class golf and fishing with an insane food and bar scene. It’s the ideal place to send off the groom in style. Here’s how to build the perfect itinerary.

Daytime Activities: Build a Solid Base

  • Go Golfing: You're near some of the best courses in the world. Book a tee time at the legendary Ocean Course on Kiawah Island or one of the many other championship courses in the area.
  • Charter a Fishing Boat: Get out on the water for some deep-sea fishing. It's a classic bachelor party move and a great way to spend the day before the night begins.
  • Brewery & Distillery Tours: Charleston has a booming craft beer and spirits scene. Check out places like Holy City Brewing or High Wire Distilling Co. for tours and tastings.

Best Bars to Get the Party Started

  • The Alley: Not just a bar, but a retro arcade with bowling lanes. Perfect for some friendly competition and casual drinks.
  • Bay Street Biergarten: A huge, modern beer hall with a massive selection of taps and great food. The communal tables are ideal for large groups.
  • Henry's on the Market: Four venues in one. It has a pub, a nightclub, a whiskey bar, and a rooftop. You can spend your whole night here.

Elevated Views: Best Rooftop Bars

  • The Vendue: A great spot to start the evening. It’s got a relaxed but upscale vibe with incredible views of the harbor.
  • Stars Rooftop & Grill Room: Right on King Street, this place is always buzzing. The 360-degree view of the city is the perfect backdrop for your first round of the night.

Late Night: Charleston's Gentlemen's Clubs

  • The King Street Cabaret: A popular downtown spot known for its classic, upscale lounge atmosphere and talented performers. A top choice for bachelor parties looking for a high-end experience.
  • The Palmetto Club: Located just outside the main downtown area, this club offers a more relaxed, traditional strip club vibe with a full bar and multiple stages.
  • Deja Vu Showgirls: A nationally recognized name with a lively party atmosphere, known for its energetic dancers and regular drink specials, making it a fun and accessible option.
