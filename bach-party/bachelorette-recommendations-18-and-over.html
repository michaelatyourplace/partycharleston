More 18+ Nightlife Tips for Charleston - Bachelorette Parties

Shem Creek Bars: Great news for under 21 guests – most bars in Shem Creek don't have someone at the door checking IDs until around 9 PM. If you arrive early, your under 21 bachelorette party guest should have no problem getting in! They'll only ID at the bar when ordering drinks, so as long as she isn't trying to order alcohol there won't be an issue. Red's Ice House and Tavern & Table offer amazing waterfront vibes.

College Scene: Charleston is a college town with venues catering to the 18+ crowd. Most downtown restaurants with bars welcome all ages until about 8 PM, when some switch to 21+ at the door. Always call ahead to confirm.

Things to Do in Charleston Under 21 Bachelorette Party Guest

Amazing activities that don't require being 21 – perfect for the whole bachelorette squad

Spa Day & Pampering

Treat the bride-to-be and crew to massages, facials, and relaxation. Many Charleston spas offer group packages perfect for bachelorette parties. Body treatments and rituals are available for guests 18+.

Beach Day Trip

Folly Beach, Sullivan's Island, and Isle of Palms are all under an hour from downtown Charleston. Rent beach chairs, paddleboards, or book a beach shuttle service for a stress-free day of sun and surf.

Boat Tours & Cruises

Explore Charleston Harbor on a sailboat, party boat, or pedal cruise. The 32-passenger party boat and Schooner Pride offer stunning sunset views and plenty of Instagram-worthy moments.

King Street Shopping

Charleston's famous shopping district features boutiques, local shops, and designer stores. Start on Upper King for art galleries and work your way down. Don't miss Kenny Flowers for matching bachelorette outfits!

Cocktail & Candle Classes

Book a private cocktail-making class (mocktails available), perfume blending at Tijon, or candle-making at Charleston Candle Co. These BYOB workshops are perfect for any age group.

Ghost Tours

Explore Charleston's haunted history with evening ghost tours. Visit the USS Yorktown paranormal tour or explore haunted graveyards downtown. Spooky fun for the whole squad!

Bachelorette Party Booking Safety Tips

Protect your squad from scams – verify before you buy!

Red Flags to Watch For
  • No physical address listed – If you can't find a real address for the business, be wary
  • Full payment required upfront – Legitimate businesses typically don't demand 100% payment before services
  • Ticket scams – Be cautious of third-party sites selling tickets to events at inflated prices or for events that don't exist
  • Bar crawl scams – Some "organized crawls" charge premium prices for things you could do yourself for free
  • Too-good-to-be-true deals – If the price seems impossibly low, investigate further
How to Verify Before Booking
  • Call the venue directly – If booking an event or activity, call the hosting location to confirm it's real instead of trusting the website alone
  • Check reviews on multiple platforms – Look for Google, Yelp, and Facebook reviews from real customers
  • Search for the business name + "scam" – A quick search can reveal if others have been burned
  • Use credit cards – Easier to dispute charges if something goes wrong
  • Trust your gut – If communication feels off or pushy, find another option
Why Charleston SC for Your Under 21 Bachelorette Party?

Charleston, South Carolina – affectionately known as "The Holy City" – is one of America's most charming destinations for bachelorette parties of all ages. This historic port city on the South Carolina coast perfectly blends Southern hospitality, stunning architecture, world-class dining, and vibrant nightlife.

With cobblestone streets, pastel-colored antebellum houses, and horse-drawn carriages, Charleston offers an Instagram-perfect backdrop for your celebration. The city is incredibly walkable, with most attractions, restaurants, and entertainment within easy reach of downtown hotels and Airbnbs near King Street.

Getting Here: Charleston International Airport (CHS) is just 25 minutes from downtown and 40 minutes to Folly Beach. Direct flights available from most major East Coast cities. Uber and Lyft are readily available throughout the area.

Best Time to Visit

Spring (April-May): Ideal weather, 70s temperatures, perfect for outdoor activities.

Fall (Sept-Nov): Mild temps, fewer crowds, gorgeous scenery.

Summer: Warm and humid but perfect for beach days. Not blowout-friendly!

Where to Stay for Your Bachelorette Weekend
Downtown Charleston

Stay on "The Peninsula" for walkable access to King Street bars, restaurants, and shops. Hotel Bennett, The Mills House, and The Vendue are popular bachelorette choices with rooftop pools and stunning views.

Folly Beach

25 minutes south of downtown with a relaxed beach vibe. Rent a vacation home for your squad and enjoy the sand by day, then cab into downtown for nightlife.

Isle of Palms

Beautiful beach rentals with private pools just steps from the sand. Perfect for larger groups wanting a beach retreat with easy access to Mount Pleasant and downtown.
