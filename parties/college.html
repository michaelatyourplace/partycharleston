Once you hit drinking age, Charleston's college weekend scene opens up completely. Here's where College of Charleston students thrive. You may find a posh.vip event at any of these places for the under 21 crowd

  • 1
    Uptown Social

    Sprawling multi-level venue with a massive rooftop, dance floor, and multiple bars. The go-to for Thursday, Friday, and Saturday nights.

  • 2
    Republic Garden & Lounge

    Club-like atmosphere with DJs, cocktails, and a sophisticated vibe on King Street.

    The Commodore

  • 6
    Ritual Rooftop Lounge

    Upscale rooftop venue with bottle service, VIP tables, and a dress code.

  • 7

    Sports bar near CofC with affordable drinks, live music, and TVs for every game. No cover.

  • 8
    The Royal American

    Live music venue and dive bar with cheap beers, an outdoor deck, and regular concerts.

  • 9
    Silver Dollar

    Classic King Street dive bar with karaoke, pool tables, and dirt-cheap drinks.

  • 10
    Bay Street Biergarten

    Outdoor beer garden with cornhole and giant Jenga — great for day drinking or early evenings before the clubs.

  • 11
    Live OnAir Club

    Mulitple story new honky tonk bar in Charleston SC.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can you go to clubs in Charleston at 18?

Most King Street clubs like Republic Garden & Lounge, Uptown Social, and Trio are 21+ only. However, Music Farm on Ann Street regularly hosts 18+ events featuring touring DJs, rock bands, and EDM acts. Honky Tonk Saloon in Ladson also offers 18+ line dancing and DJs. Check venue calendars for specific 18+ event dates.

What can you do in Charleston 18-20?

Charleston offers plenty for the young adult crowd including beaches (Folly Beach, Sullivan's Island, Isle of Palms), private boat charters, candle making workshops, upscale dining, King Street shopping, beach yoga, and outdoor activities at Shem Creek and Mount Pleasant Pier. You can also enjoy 18+ shows at Music Farm.

Is Charleston a party school?

Yes — largely due to the College of Charleston's location directly in downtown's historic district, steps from King Street, with thousands of students from CofC, MUSC, and Trident Technical College creating a vibrant scene with bars, clubs, and legendary house parties.

What are the best 21+ bars in Charleston for college students?

Top picks include Uptown Social, Republic Garden & Lounge, Trio Nightclub, The Commodore, Burns Alley, The Royal American, Silver Dollar, and Bay Street Biergarten.

Are there mocktail bars in Charleston?

Charleston doesn't have dedicated mocktail-only bars, but many upscale venues offer great zero-proof options, including The Gin Joint, Citrus Club, Little Palm Bar, and Basic Kitchen.
