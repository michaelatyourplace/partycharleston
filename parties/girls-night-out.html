FAQ

What exactly is Girls Night Out The Show?

Girls Night Out The Show is a live private entertainment experience in Charleston, SC featuring Michael as the headline performer. It's a crowd-interactive performance — with music, games, comedy, and personalized moments — held at your private venue. Think live show meets celebration, not a bar night.

How do I book Girls Night Out Charleston with Michael?

Visit michaelatyourplace.com to check availability and book your date. Weekend dates during bachelorette season (March through October) fill fast — book at least 2–4 weeks in advance for best availability.

Where does the show take place?

The show comes to your location — Airbnb, vacation rental, private home, or event space anywhere in the Charleston metro area including downtown Charleston, Mount Pleasant, Isle of Palms, Folly Beach, and Sullivan's Island. You provide the space, Michael provides the show.

How large does the group need to be?

Girls Night Out The Show works for groups from 8 to 40 guests. The sweet spot is 10–20 for the most interactive experience. Larger groups can be accommodated — contact for custom group pricing.

What other girls night out activities pair well with the show?

The show works best as the evening's centerpiece. Pair it with dinner on King Street beforehand (Republic, The Macintosh, or The Ordinary), then hit rooftop bars like Prohibition or Uptown Social after. Daytime options like spa at Charleston Place, harbor cruises, or candle-making at Candlefish pair well for full-day itineraries.

Is the show appropriate for all ages?

Yes — Girls Night Out The Show is designed for all adult ages. Whether your group is 21 or 51, the show is built to entertain and delight. Mixed-age friend groups, moms celebrating birthdays, divorce parties, girls trips — all are welcome.
