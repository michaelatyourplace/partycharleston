Girls Night Out Ideas Charleston

Looking for unforgettable girls night out ideas Charleston has to offer? Whether you're planning a ladies night out in Charleston with your best friends, celebrating a bachelorette party, or simply want to explore the best girls night activities Charleston SC provides, you've come to the right place. From luxurious spa experiences and dessert tastings to rooftop cocktails and interactive adventures, Charleston offers endless options for an amazing night out with the girls.

1. Indulge at The Spa at Charleston Place

For the ultimate girls night out ideas Charleston style, treat yourselves to a luxurious spa experience at The Spa at Charleston Place. This award-winning European spa, named "Best City Hotel Spa" by Travel + Leisure readers, offers world-class treatments in an elegant downtown setting perfect for a ladies night Charleston celebration.

Book a group spa package that includes couples' suites (perfect for pairs of friends), therapeutic massages with hot Himalayan salt stones, rejuvenating facials with organic botanicals, and luxurious manicures and pedicures. After your treatments, relax in the serene relaxation lounge or enjoy the rooftop saltwater pool with stunning city views. The spa's tranquil atmosphere provides the perfect escape for reconnecting with your girlfriends.

Address: 205 Meeting Street, Charleston, SC 29401
Phone: (843) 937-8522
Email: Spa@CharlestonPlace.com
Hours: Mon-Fri 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM, Sat-Sun 8:00 AM - 7:00 PM

2. Dessert Tasting at Weltons Tiny Bakeshop

Experience one of the sweetest Charleston girls night out activities with a dessert tasting adventure at Weltons Tiny Bakeshop. This cult-favorite bakery creates European-style pastries using locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients and wood-fired ovens, making it a must-visit for any ladies night out Charleston itinerary.

Sample their famous Salted Honey Pie (featured on national TV), flaky kouign-amann, Portuguese egg tarts, twice-baked croissants with candied orange, and olive oil almond cake. The talented chef-owners Hannah and Zachary Welton trained at acclaimed restaurants and bring incredible creativity to every pastry. Arrive early as this tiny gem sells out quickly, and enjoy your treats at their outdoor alleyway tables while catching up with your crew.

Address: 682 King Street, Charleston, SC 29403
Phone: (843) 666-7277
Hours: Wed-Sun 8:00 AM - 1:00 PM (or until sold out)

3. Southern Biscuit Tasting at Callie's Hot Little Biscuit

No list of girls night out ideas Charleston would be complete without Callie's Hot Little Biscuit. This award-winning spot serves handmade biscuits crafted without any machinery, making it perfect for a delicious girls night activity Charleston style. Their biscuits have been featured on Food Network and ship nationwide due to their popularity.

Order a biscuit flight to share with your girlfriends and sample both savory options (cheddar and chive, country ham, black pepper bacon) and sweet varieties (blackberry jam, honey drizzle). The biscuits are made with cream cheese for an extra-flaky texture. Grab your order to-go and enjoy them while strolling King Street or head to nearby Marion Square to sit and savor every bite while planning the rest of your evening.

City Market Location: 188 Meeting Street, Charleston, SC 29401
Phone: (843) 737-5159

Hours: Daily 8:00 AM - 2:00 PM

4. Experience Live Music & Rooftop Views at Henry's On The Market

For girls night out Charleston entertainment, Henry's On The Market delivers multiple floors of fun. This historic venue established in 1932 features a lively rooftop deck with city views, a cozy Whiskey Lounge with speakeasy vibes, and live music seven nights a week - making it one of the top ladies night Charleston destinations.

Each floor offers a different vibe from intimate jazz to energetic bands, so you can bar-hop without leaving the building. The rooftop provides sunset cocktails with incredible views of Charleston's skyline. Try their famous She Crab Soup and stay for the evening entertainment. It's the perfect spot to dance, laugh, and celebrate with your girlfriends.

Address: 54 North Market Street, Charleston, SC 29401
Phone: (843) 723-4363
Hours: Daily 11:00 AM - 2:00 AM

5. Craft Cocktails at Award-Winning Speakeasies

Charleston's sophisticated cocktail scene offers perfect girls night activities Charleston SC style. Visit award-winning speakeasies and craft cocktail bars throughout downtown for an evening of expertly-mixed drinks in glamorous settings. These venues have won national recognition for their cocktail programs and ambiance.

Start at Prohibition for their famous bacon maple old fashioned and live jazz in a 1920s-inspired setting, then explore other craft cocktail destinations like The Gin Joint or Vintage Lounge. Many bars feature talented female bartenders who create innovative cocktails with local ingredients. The intimate atmosphere and creative drinks make for perfect conversation and Instagram-worthy moments with your crew.

6. Waterfront Wine & Sunset Views

Charleston's stunning harbor locations provide beautiful settings for ladies night out Charleston experiences. Head to waterfront restaurants and rooftop bars for breathtaking sunset views paired with excellent wine selections. Watch the sun set over Charleston Harbor while enjoying coastal breezes and quality time with your girlfriends.

Many venues offer extensive wine lists featuring selections from around the world, plus local craft beverages. The combination of gorgeous water views, delicious wines, and Charleston's famous church steeples silhouetted against colorful skies creates an unforgettable evening. Reserve a waterfront table in advance, especially during peak season.

7. Moonlight Ghost Tours

Add thrills to your girls night out ideas Charleston adventure with an evening ghost tour through the city's haunted historic district. Charleston ranks as one of America's most haunted cities, and moonlight tours blend history, mystery, and entertainment perfectly for groups of girlfriends seeking something different.

Professional guides lead 90-120 minute walking tours through moonlit graveyards, haunted churches, and historic homes while sharing spine-tingling tales of Charleston's past. Tours like those offered by Bulldog Tours and Nightly Spirits provide engaging storytelling and surprising scares. It's an interactive way to explore Charleston's fascinating history while bonding over shared thrills with your friends.

8. King Street Shopping & Bar Crawl

Combine retail therapy with cocktails on historic King Street for a classic Charleston girls night out experience. This walkable street spans over three miles with distinct shopping districts, trendy bars, and restaurants, making it ideal for an evening of exploring with your girlfriends.

Start at Lower King for upscale boutiques, browse Upper King for vintage finds and local designers, then pop into stylish cocktail bars along the way. Try spots like Bar Felix for espresso martinis or Little Palm for tropical drinks. The pedestrian-friendly street makes it easy to window shop, try on outfits, and enjoy drinks all in one memorable night.

9. Wine Tasting & Blending Classes

For sophisticated girls night out ideas Charleston offers, book a wine blending or tasting class where you'll become vintners for the evening. Expert sommeliers guide you through tasting multiple varietals while teaching about wine profiles, food pairings, and blending techniques.

Create your own custom wine blend to bottle and take home as a keepsake from your ladies night Charleston adventure. These intimate classes appeal to wine enthusiasts and novices alike, providing education in a fun, social setting. Several wine bars and hotels throughout Charleston offer these experiences - perfect for bachelorette parties or birthday celebrations.

10. Drag Shows & Drag Brunch Entertainment

Experience Charleston's vibrant entertainment scene with a drag show or drag brunch - one of the most fun girls night activities Charleston SC style. These high-energy performances feature talented drag queens, live singing, comedy routines, and audience interaction that guarantees an unforgettable evening.

Venues throughout Charleston host drag brunches with bottomless mimosas and incredible performances, plus evening shows with dinner and cocktails. Expect spectacular costumes, powerful vocals, hilarious comedy, and plenty of opportunities for photos with the performers. It's an evening filled with laughter, positivity, and memories your group will talk about for years.

11. Charleston Harbor Sunset Cruise

Take your girls night out Charleston celebration onto the water with a harbor cruise or sunset sail. Options range from relaxing dolphin tours to party boats with music and dancing. Seeing Charleston's skyline from the water during golden hour provides magical photo opportunities and a unique perspective of the Holy City.

Most cruises last 90-120 minutes and many allow you to bring your own beverages. You'll likely spot dolphins playing in the harbor, catch stunning views of Rainbow Row and the Arthur Ravenel Jr. Bridge, and enjoy coastal breezes. It's especially popular for bachelorette parties and milestone birthday celebrations with your closest girlfriends.

Planning Your Perfect Charleston Girls Night Out

Charleston truly excels when it comes to girls night out ideas Charleston style. Whether you prefer pampering spa experiences, sweet dessert tastings, rooftop cocktails, or waterfront adventures, the Holy City delivers memorable experiences for every type of friend group.

Most downtown Charleston venues are within walking distance, making it easy to combine multiple activities into one epic ladies night out Charleston adventure. Many restaurants and bars offer ladies night promotions and happy hour specials throughout the week. Charleston's Southern hospitality ensures warm welcomes everywhere you go.

Remember to make reservations in advance for popular spots like The Spa at Charleston Place and Weltons Tiny Bakeshop, especially on weekends. Arrive early at Callie's Hot Little Biscuit as they often sell out by mid-morning. For the best experience, plan a route that keeps your group in one area to maximize time together rather than driving across town.

From bachelorette parties to casual catch-ups with your besties, these girls night activities Charleston SC style guarantee an amazing time in one of America's most charming and welcoming cities. Cheers to unforgettable nights with your favorite people!
