Charleston After Work

Your guide to the best happy hours, networking spots, and team outings in the Holy City.

When the clock hits 5:00 PM, Charleston truly comes alive. The city's vibrant culture extends well beyond tourism, offering a dynamic after-work scene for professionals looking to unwind, network, or celebrate a team win. From sophisticated rooftop bars with skyline views to casual waterfront spots perfect for decompressing, there's a happy hour for every industry and every mood.

Downtown's Happy Hour Corridor

The heart of the after-work scene beats strongest on the peninsula. Professionals from the city's legal, financial, and tech sectors flock to the sophisticated bars and restaurants along King Street and East Bay Street. Rooftop bars like The Vendue or Fiat Lux at Hotel Bennett offer breathtaking views and craft cocktails, perfect for impressing clients or celebrating a successful quarter. For a more grounded but equally chic experience, cocktail lounges like The Gin Joint or Proof provide an intimate setting for quality conversation and expertly made drinks. These spots are not just for drinking; they are buzzing hubs of networking where connections are made and ideas are born.

Waterfront Unwind: Mount Pleasant & Shem Creek

For those looking to escape the downtown hustle, a short drive over the Ravenel Bridge to Mount Pleasant is a must. The iconic Shem Creek is lined with bars and restaurants offering unbeatable waterfront views. Places like Tavern & Table and Saltwater Cowboys are local favorites, where you can watch the shrimp boats come in and dolphins play in the creek while enjoying a cold beer and fresh seafood. It’s the ultimate Charleston way to de-stress, offering a mini-vacation vibe that's perfect for casual team outings and relaxed happy hours with colleagues.

The Local's Choice: West Ashley & Avondale

If you're looking for a more laid-back, authentic Charleston experience, the Avondale neighborhood in West Ashley is your spot. This trendy area boasts an eclectic mix of bars and restaurants with a distinctly local feel. It’s the perfect place for a low-key gathering after a long day. With a variety of options all within walking distance of each other, Avondale allows for easy bar-hopping and provides a more casual atmosphere for co-workers to connect on a personal level. It's where you go to celebrate finishing a big project or just to enjoy a well-deserved drink with the people you spend your work week with.
