The Ultimate Charleston Girls Trip Guide

Discover the 10 Best Places for an Unforgettable Girlfriends Getaway in Charleston, SC

Planning a Charleston girls trip is the perfect way to celebrate friendship, mark special occasions, or simply escape for a weekend of Southern charm and unforgettable memories. Whether you're organizing a bachelorette party, celebrating a milestone birthday, or gathering your closest friends for a much-needed getaway, Charleston offers the ideal blend of historic beauty, culinary excellence, beachside relaxation, and vibrant nightlife. From sunrise strolls along cobblestone streets to sunset cruises on the harbor, this coastal gem delivers experiences that will have your group laughing, bonding, and creating stories you'll reminisce about for years to come. Get ready to discover why thousands of women choose Charleston for their ultimate girlfriends escape.

Top 10 Must-Visit Destinations

Shopping & Culture

Charleston City Market

Step into Charleston's historic heart at the City Market, where local artisans showcase handwoven sweetgrass baskets, Southern crafts, and unique treasures. This iconic four-block marketplace is perfect for finding authentic Charleston souvenirs while soaking up the vibrant atmosphere of the Holy City. Your girls will love browsing the countless vendor stalls filled with jewelry, art, and local delicacies.

Visit Charleston City Market
Adventure & Thrills

Walking Ghost Tours

Experience Charleston's spooky side with an evening ghost tour through the city's haunted historic district. Led by knowledgeable guides, you'll hear chilling tales of restless spirits, tragic love stories, and centuries-old mysteries while walking cobblestone streets by lantern light. It's an entertaining and slightly eerie way to explore Charleston's dark history with your crew.

Book a Ghost Tour
Beach & Relaxation

Folly Beach

Known as "The Edge of America," Folly Beach offers laid-back vibes, pristine sandy shores, and perfect waves just 15 minutes from downtown Charleston. Spend your day sunbathing, paddle boarding, or sipping cocktails at beachfront bars. The iconic Folly Beach Pier provides stunning photo opportunities, while the local surf shops and casual eateries complete the quintessential beach town experience.

Explore Folly Beach
Nightlife & Dancing

The Citrus Club

Get ready to dance the night away at The Citrus Club, Charleston's premier nightlife destination on Upper King Street. This multi-level venue features a rooftop bar with skyline views, multiple dance floors with different music genres, and an energetic atmosphere perfect for bachelorette parties and girls' nights out. The club's vibrant energy and top-notch DJs make it the ultimate spot to celebrate.

Visit The Citrus Club
Culinary & Wine

Wine Tasting Class

Elevate your Charleston girls trip with a sophisticated wine tasting experience where you'll sample exquisite wines while learning about varietals, regions, and pairing techniques. Local wine bars and tasting rooms offer intimate group experiences perfect for bonding over bold reds, crisp whites, and sparkling rosés. Many venues can customize private tastings for bachelorette groups and special celebrations.

Book Wine Tasting
Sweet Treats

Welton's Tiny Bakery

Indulge your sweet tooth at Welton's Tiny Bakery, a charming bakery serving up heavenly pastries, cookies, and seasonal treats made with locally sourced ingredients. From flaky croissants to decadent brownies and creative seasonal offerings, every bite is Instagram-worthy and absolutely delicious. Arrive early because these handcrafted goodies sell out fast!

Visit Welton's
Breakfast & Brunch

Callie's Hot Little Biscuit

Start your day right at Callie's Hot Little Biscuit, a beloved Charleston institution famous for their buttery, flaky biscuits piled high with Southern-inspired toppings. From the classic Calhoun (buttermilk biscuit with aged cheddar and sausage gravy) to sweet options with jam and honey butter, these handmade biscuits are the ultimate comfort food to fuel your Charleston adventures.

Visit Callie's Biscuit
Historic Landmark

Rainbow Row

Capture the perfect group photo at Rainbow Row, Charleston's most photographed street featuring 13 colorful Georgian row houses painted in pastel hues. Located on East Bay Street near The Battery, these iconic 18th-century homes create a stunning backdrop for your girls trip memories. Golden hour lighting makes the colors pop even more beautifully in photos.

Learn About Rainbow Row
Scenic Experience

Sunset Boat Tour

Experience Charleston's breathtaking beauty from the water with a sunset harbor cruise. Sip champagne with your girls while watching the sun paint the sky in brilliant oranges and pinks behind Charleston's historic skyline and waterfront mansions. These tours often include narration about Charleston's maritime history, views of Fort Sumter, and dolphins playing in the wake.

Book Sunset Cruise
Culinary Adventure

Food and Wine Tours

Savor Charleston's renowned culinary scene with a guided food and wine tour through the historic district. Sample Lowcountry favorites like she-crab soup, shrimp and grits, and fresh oysters while visiting award-winning restaurants, hidden gems, and local markets. Expert guides share the stories behind Charleston's food culture while you taste your way through the city with your crew.

Book Food Tour

Honorable Mentions

While our top 10 list covers the essentials, Charleston has so much more to offer! Here are additional spots that deserve recognition for your girls trip:

  • Michael At Your Place Charleston - fun show to energize your group at midday or night
  • King Street Shopping - Premier shopping district with boutiques and national brands
  • Magnolia Plantation & Gardens - Beautiful historic gardens perfect for photos
  • Waterfront Park - Iconic pineapple fountain and harbor views
  • Upper King Street - Trendy restaurants, bars, and nightlife scene
  • Charleston Tea Plantation - Only tea plantation in North America
  • The Darling Oyster Bar - Fresh seafood and lively atmosphere
  • Rooftop Bars (Market Pavilion, The Vendue) - Cocktails with skyline views
  • Second Sunday on King Street - Monthly street festival (seasonal)
  • Charleston Farmers Market - Local produce and artisan goods (Saturday mornings)
