Nightlife Activities In Charleston SC

While exploring the downtown core, particularly the famous Upper King Street, here are some alternative experiences for those looking for a night time idea or activity in the Charleston SC area.

When you think of a "party" in the Lowcountry, one place immediately comes to mind: **Downtown Charleston**. This historic peninsula is the undeniable, magnetic center of all nightlife, drawing in crowds from every corner of the region. While the greater Charleston area, including parts of North Charleston, has its own entertainment options, they exist as satellites to the main star.
