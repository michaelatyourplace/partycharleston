The 7 Best Rooftop Bars Near King Street

In a city as beautiful as Charleston, drinking at street level is a missed opportunity. This is the "Holy City," a place famous for its protected skyline—a stunning panorama of historic church steeples that can't be obstructed. This law makes Charleston's rooftop bars some of the most charming and unique in the country.

But not all rooftops are created equal. Some are high-energy party scenes, others are quiet, ultra-luxe lounges. We've ranked the best of the best in and around the King Street area to help you find the perfect view for your vibe, whether it's a classy start or a wild night.

The Rooftop Rundown: Our Top 7 Picks

Here's our definitive list, from high-energy parties to classy cocktail spots, ranked for your perfect King Street Bar Crawl.

1. The Vendue (The Rooftop Bar)

Vibe: The Classic, Can't-Miss View.

This isn't just *a* bar, it's *the* rooftop bar. Consistently voted "Best Rooftop Bar" by locals for years, The Vendue (located just off East Bay, a quick walk from Lower King) offers the single best panoramic view of Charleston. You get the harbor, the Ravenel Bridge, Waterfront Park, and the entire historic skyline. It's a massive space with two levels, multiple bars, and a mix of tourists and locals. It gets packed, but the vibe is "upscale social" rather than a "wild party."

  • Best For: The first-time visitor, impressing a date, bachelorette party kick-offs.
  • Drink This: Their specialty frozen cocktails or a classic glass of rosé.
  • Pro-Tip: It's 21+ only after 5 PM. Get there before sunset to snag a prime seat, as tables are first-come, first-served and fill up fast.

2. Stars Rooftop & Grill Room

Vibe: The 360° View & Classic Feel.

Located right on Mid-King, Stars is a Charleston classic. The "Grill Room" downstairs is a nice restaurant, but the real prize is the 360-degree view from the rooftop. It's one of the few spots where you can see *all* of King Street from above. The bar itself is wood-paneled and has a more "adult," classic feel. It's a fantastic spot for a pre-dinner drink at sunset or as a slightly more relaxed alternative to the Upper King chaos. It's classy, reliable, and the views are undeniable.

  • Best For: Sunset drinks, a 360-degree view, starting a "classy" night.
  • Drink This: A local craft beer on tap or a classic Old Fashioned.
  • Pro-Tip: The rooftop menu is limited, so plan on eating downstairs if you're hungry. The rooftop is 21+ after 9 PM.

3. Ritual Rooftop Restaurant & Lounge

Vibe: The See-and-Be-Seen Club Scene.

If The Vendue is classy, Ritual is a *party*. This is one of Upper King's newest and hottest rooftops, located on top of the Moxy hotel. It's a massive, Tulum-inspired space with DJs, a dance floor, VIP sections, and a high-energy "club" atmosphere. This is where you go late-night to dance and be seen. The views of Upper King are great, but you're here for the scene, the music, and the energy.

  • Best For: A late-night party, dancing, large groups, bottle service.
  • Drink This: Their signature craft cocktails or bottle service.
  • Pro-Tip: Expect a line and a dress code (no shorts for guys) on weekends. This is a "late-night" spot, so the party gets going after 10 PM.

4. Uptown Social

Vibe: The College Party Rooftop.

While known as a massive sports bar and mega-party spot, Uptown's rooftop patio is one of the best and biggest on Upper King. It's not a "sit and sip" spot; it's a direct, open-air extension of the party downstairs. It has its own bar, high-top tables, and is almost always packed with the 21+ college crowd. If you're with a big group, want a high-energy night, and love the idea of a house-party-on-a-roof, this is your go-to.

  • Best For: The college crowd, game days, big groups, a casual party.
  • Drink This: A frozen drink (their "Frozé" is famous) or a bucket of beers.
  • Pro-Tip: This is one of the most casual rooftops. No need to overdress. It's all about fun, high-volume energy.

5. Fiat Lux (at Hotel Bennett)

Vibe: The Ultra-Luxe, Exclusive Escape.

This is the most "luxurious" rooftop on the list. Located in the opulent Hotel Bennett overlooking Marion Square, Fiat Lux is a stunningly beautiful (and very expensive) cocktail bar. The vibe is quiet, exclusive, and incredibly chic, with a gorgeous central bar and cozy fireplace seating. You're not "partying" here—you're sipping a $25 craft cocktail and feeling like royalty. The views of the park are unmatched.

  • Best For: A *very* special occasion, impressing parents, a quiet, romantic drink.
  • Drink This: A perfectly made classic, like a Martini or a glass of real champagne.
  • Pro-Tip: Dress the part. You will feel very out of place in party clothes. Reservations are a good idea.

6. Élevé Rooftop Restaurant & Lounge

Vibe: The Whimsical, Artsy Garden Party.

Perched atop the Grand Bohemian Hotel, right at the corner of Market and King, Élevé is a visual feast. It's a whimsical, art-filled space that feels like a magical, hidden garden. It's more of a restaurant than a bar, but the terrace is a perfect spot for a glass of wine and a deep conversation. It's an ideal "Lower King" starting point before heading north, offering a dose of colorful, imaginative charm.

  • Best For: A creative date, an art lover, a bachelorette brunch.
  • Drink This: A sparkling wine or one of their floral craft cocktails.
  • Pro-Tip: This is a great spot for a group brunch or an early-evening drink, as it's more relaxed than the late-night party spots.

7. Citrus Club (at The Dewberry)

Vibe: The Mid-Century Modern Hotspot.

Tucked away in The Dewberry hotel, this rooftop bar offers some of the highest and most unique views of the city, looking out over Marion Square from the north. It boasts a cool, mid-century modern decor and is famous for its bright, citrus-themed craft cocktails. It's trendy, exclusive, and has a "private club" feel. It's notoriously hard to get into, which only adds to its allure.

  • Best For: A trendy crowd, cocktail connoisseurs, an "if you can get in" thrill.
  • Drink This: Their signature "Citrus Club" cocktail.
  • Pro-Tip: Reservations are almost essential. They are released 14 days in advance. Hotel guests get priority, so if you're not staying there, plan ahead.

The Verdict: How to Plan Your Rooftop Crawl

You can structure an entire King Street Bar Crawl just by hopping between rooftops. Here's a quick plan based on your mood:

The "Classy" Crawl

Start at Fiat Lux for that ultra-luxe drink, move to Élevé for the artsy vibe, and end at The Vendue for the iconic sunset harbor view. This is a pricey but unforgettable evening.

The "Party" Crawl

Start at Stars for the 360° sunset view, head to Uptown Social to join the high-energy college party, and then finish your night at Ritual to dance until closing.

No matter which you choose, you'll be getting a view of the Holy City that most people miss—and that's a perfect way to start any night.
